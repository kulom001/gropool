About Gropool

Gropol is the brain child of Om and Soham Kulkarni, and was created to help their grandma and extended family during the coronavirus. They came up with Gropool by combining the two words "grocery" and "carpool". Their main goal is to decrease the chance of getting the coronavirus by having close friends, neighbors, and family take turns going to the grocery store, which is one of the most likely places you can get the coronavirus. This will decrease the amount of grocery trips the average American has to take, and also reduces the amount if people at grocery stores. Big thanks to the Shooting Stars Foundation for funding.
